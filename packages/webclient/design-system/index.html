Colors

Apply the rules of heraldic tinging. There are the metals gold (yellow) and silver (white) as well as the colors blue, red, green and black. Metals must not border on metals. Colors must not border on colors.

Proven combinations are red/silver, blue/silver, black/silber, green/silver and black/gold.
